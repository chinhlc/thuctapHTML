Product

Product Organization

Support all Magento product types. Search products quickly by name, ID, SKU or barcode.

Barcode Support

Assign existing barcodes to products and print labels. Scan barcode to add products to customers' carts.

Multi-warehouse Inventory

Inventory synced in real time between stores. Check stock of each store and transfer stock directly in POS.

AI Product Recommendation

Combined with Facial Recognition technology (VeriFace), ConnectPOS helps store associates deliver a better personalized experience with products recommended exclusively for each customer.

Checkout

3-Step Checkout

Serve more customers in the same amount of time with quick checkout. Easy to use and navigate, reduce errors during peak times.

Custom Sale

Create items not yet available on Magento store to add to the cart quickly.

Promotions

Discount by percentage or number on selected items or the whole cart. Support Magento coupon codes, gift cards and reward points..

Multiple Tax Levels

Synchronized tax settings with Magento ensure consistency. Taxes are calculated in POS applying Magento tax rules - correct results are guaranteed even in Offline Mode.

Order Operations

Add notes, save and retrieve on-hold orders, send cart to wishlist for online purchase later - ConnectPOS has it all!

Shipping

Synchronized shipping methods with Magento. Shipping fees are calculated and updated directly in POS. Edit and add shipping addresses.

Receipts

Customize receipt templates for different purposes, with your logo, website and store address. Email or print receipts after a sale.

Check Out on Multiple Devices

Sell from a stationary position, on the counter with web POS, or around the store and on the go with iPad POS. Second screen enables information transparency and reduces errors upon checkout.

Payments

Custom Payments

Create custom payment options to accept cheques, or payments made with an external device

Integrated Payments

ConnectPOS is integrated with Tyro (web POS and iPad POS), PayPal Here, Payment Express (iPad POS), Braintree and Moneris (coming soon).

Split Tenders

Accept two or more payment types in a transaction, i.e. credit card and cash, or multiple credit cards.

Layaway (Partial Payment)

Allow your customers to make deposits or layaways and pay the rest later with any method.

Refund & Exchange

Refund using original payment method, refund to reward points or exchange items for dissatisfied customers.

Multi-currency

View order values and accept payments in different currencies.

Customer

Customer Database

Synchronized customer database with Magento. Create, edit and add customers. Check out as guest.

Customer Loyalty

Award loyal customers with coupons, reward points and discounts and keep them coming back for more.

Wish-list

Send products to customers' online carts or wishlists for them to buy online later

Customer App

Customers have all the freedom to search for information they need and check out on their own with Customer App by ConnectPOS. No more queuing and waiting!

Report System

Central Data Management

View reports from your offline and online stores in one place to get a better view of your business.

Cash Float

Easily monitor all cash register adjustments and staff changes throughout the day.

Diverse Reports

20 different reports - all the information needed for store owners and cashiers

Customizable Dashboard

Get all critical metrics in a clear dashboard with advanced options and built-in filters.

Export Reports

Export data to CSV file for further analysis or send reports to your accountants.

Insight Distillation

Analyze and gain insight into your store’s growth, from best-selling products, staff performance to customer behaviors.

Multi-store Management

Multi Stores and Multi Warehouses

Create new stores and assign a warehouse to each store. Manage orders and inventory across sales channels.

Add registers

Add registers to sell more during peak season and easily deactivate them when they're not needed.

Translation Tool

You can change the language of the user interface and the receipt into the local language.

Real Time Synchronization

Sync your physical stores in real time with your online business, avoid selling out-of-stock products.
